Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Bigger or Not? ("the App") is published by BasikCode. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Local Preferences

The App stores gameplay preferences (best streak, sound-effects toggle, unit preference, question order seed) on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and contains no personally identifiable information.

1.2 Advertising

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve and measure ads. For details, see Google's Advertising Privacy Policy.

Where required by law (e.g., in the EEA), the App requests your consent before serving personalized ads via Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). You can withdraw consent at any time through your device's ad settings.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account registration or login
  • No name, email, phone number, or other personal information
  • No location data
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • No analytics SDK beyond what AdMob provides

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us.

5. Data Retention

Local preferences are stored until you clear the App's data or uninstall it. Ad-related data retention is governed by Google's policies.

6. Your Rights

You can reset all local data at any time via Settings > Reset Progress in the App. To opt out of personalized ads, visit your device's Ads settings.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
